About Us

Your trusted partner in optimizing healthcare innovation

Mission

At Inlightened, our mission is to drive transformative healthcare innovation by seamlessly connecting companies with a diverse array of experts. We are committed to facilitating informed, responsible advancements that enhance patient care and address the pressing challenges faced by the healthcare industry.

Values

We are dedicated to promoting diversity, equality, and fairness. By democratizing access to curated and vetted healthcare professionals, we foster a culture that values specialized knowledge, transparency, and meaningful innovation.

Story

Inlightened was founded in 2019 by Shelli Pavone and Michelle Higginson in Boston, Massachusetts. We were acquired by LocumTenens.com, part of the Jackson Healthcare® family of companies, in August of 2023, and continue to operate as an independent entity. Inlightened has experienced significant growth in recent years, but one thing remains consistent: We are committed to changing the way healthcare innovation works — or historically hasn’t worked — by prioritizing diversity, transparency, and quality.

The Inlightened difference

Inlightened provides exclusive access to a curated network of highly-specialized healthcare professionals, each committed to pioneering advancements in their fields. Our platform simplifies and automates the traditionally cumbersome tasks of vetting, contracting, scheduling, and payments, freeing organizations to focus on harnessing critical insights to tackle the most urgent challenges in healthcare.
